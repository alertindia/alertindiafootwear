About Alert India Footwear

Alert India Footwear is a premier manufacturer of high-end, fancy footwear for women. We are dedicated to the art of shoemaking, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create pieces that are both beautiful and comfortable.

At Alert India Footwear, we specialize in designing and producing our own exclusive collections while also serving as a trusted manufacturing partner for reputed national footwear brands based in Kolkata and Bengaluru. With state-of-the-art facilities and a strong focus on quality, innovation, and timely delivery, we have built long-standing relationships in the industry. Our proven expertise in large-scale footwear manufacturing makes us the ideal partner for brands seeking reliability, consistent quality, and stylish designs. By combining craftsmanship with modern production capabilities, we help our partners strengthen their brand presence while ensuring customer satisfaction.
